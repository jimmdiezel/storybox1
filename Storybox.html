Old Kent Society

Welcome Pack

Welcome to the Old Kent Society. Our organisation was created to analyse social deprivation within Southwark. We think that we can begin to restrict the market for exploitation and establish an economy for social development by monitoring commercial activity. We want to build consensus around our definition of the essential elements of a fair society and then use these qualities to assess commercial enterprise. We intend to establish an internationally approved statistical classification for social cohesion/productivity and hold the belief that this framework of widely held principles and values will provide humanity with the opportunity to build an alliance against conflict and deprivation.

Classification Systems

Classification systems stand at the heart of key industries like medicine and banking as commercial enterprises exploit international classification systems in pursuit of profit. The pharmaceutical industry relies on the Diagnostic and Statistical Manual to distribute psychoactive substances throughout the world while the finance sector has depended on the credit rating system to determine the profitable rates of interest available on public debt. We believe it is time to develop a statistical classification to measure the value of commercial engagement for ordinary working people.
